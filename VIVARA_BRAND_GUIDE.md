## Brand Overview

### What is Vivara?

**Vivara** is a revolutionary iOS video editing application that uses advanced person segmentation AI to make people stand out in vivid, vibrant color while transforming the background into elegant grayscale. The result is a striking cinematic effect where subjects radiate with color while the world around them fades to monochrome.

### Name Origin

**Vivara** combines:
- **Viv** — from "vivid," meaning intensely deep, bright, and alive
- **Ara** — from "aura," the distinctive atmosphere or quality that surrounds a person

Together: *Your vivid aura. Your radiant presence.*

### Target Audience

- **Primary:** Content creators, social media enthusiasts (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube)
- **Secondary:** Videographers, filmmakers, digital artists
- **Age range:** 16-35 years old
- **Tech-savvy:** iPhone users comfortable with creative apps

### Brand Positioning

Vivara positions itself as the **premium, easy-to-use** solution for creating professional-quality selective color videos on mobile. We make advanced AI technology accessible to everyone.

**Key differentiators:**
- Real-time person segmentation (no manual masking)
- Professional results with one tap
- Optimized for social media sharing
- Clean, intuitive interface

### Accessibility Guidelines

**Contrast Ratios (WCAG 2.1 AA Compliance):**
- Body text (Charcoal #374151 on White): 10.7:1 ✓
- Electric Coral on White: 3.4:1 (use for large text only)
- Vivid Purple on White: 5.1:1 ✓
- Always test color combinations for accessibility

**Color Blind Considerations:**
- Avoid relying solely on coral/gray distinction
- Use icons, labels, and patterns in addition to color
- Test with color blindness simulators

## Usage Guidelines

### Logo Usage

**DO:**
- ✓ Maintain clear space (minimum 24px around logo)
- ✓ Use provided logo files (never recreate)
- ✓ Scale proportionally
- ✓ Use on white, light gray, or dark backgrounds with appropriate version
- ✓ Use vector formats (SVG/PDF) when possible

**DON'T:**
- ✗ Stretch, squash, or distort the logo
- ✗ Change colors (except approved dark mode version)
- ✗ Add effects (extra shadows, outlines beyond brand glow)
- ✗ Place on busy backgrounds where legibility is compromised
- ✗ Rotate the logo
- ✗ Separate "Viv" and "ara" onto different lines

### Minimum Sizes

- **Wordmark logo:** 100px wide minimum
- **App icon:** 60×60px minimum (iOS home screen)
- **Favicon:** 32×32px

### Clear Space

Maintain a clear space around the logo equal to the height of the letter "a" in "ara" on all sides.

```
    ┌─a─┐
    │   │
  a │ V │ a
    │   │
    └─a─┘
```

### Dark Mode Adaptations

**Logo on dark backgrounds:**
- "Viv" gradient: Slightly brighter variants
  - Electric Coral: #FF8787
  - Vivid Purple: #9B7AFF
- "ara": Light gray (#E5E7EB) instead of charcoal

**UI on dark mode:**
- Background: #1F2937 (dark gray)
- Cards: #374151 (charcoal)
- Text: #F9FAFB (near white)
- Maintain brand color accents (coral, purple, cyan)
- Glow effects can be slightly more prominent
